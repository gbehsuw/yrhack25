About Us

My name is Mithulan Nanthakumar and I am a grade 11 student at Richmond Hill High School. Something my parents taught my sister and I when young was the importance of financial management. However, this is complicated for students and adults alike who have not been exposed to helpful tools. My goal is to create a tool that helps people of all ages learn to track their spending in a simple manner.

The Product

Receiptify is a tool to solve just that problem.

Simply upload links to receipts you have and observe helpful and insightful data.

With this information, identifying large and small areas of spendature by category becomes incredibly easy.

If you need help getting an image for your receipts, I suggest using postimage!
